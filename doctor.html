About Dr. Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a board-certified cardiologist with extensive experience in diagnosing and treating heart conditions. She completed her medical degree at Johns Hopkins University and her residency at Massachusetts General Hospital.

Dr. Johnson specializes in preventive cardiology, helping patients reduce their risk of heart disease through lifestyle changes and medical interventions. She believes in a patient-centered approach, taking time to understand each individual's unique needs and concerns.
